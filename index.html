Hangzhou — Paradise on the Earth!

Sitting on the southeastern coastal line, Hangzhou is a medium-sized city. During the Song Dynasty, the city was the capital of the dynasty.

Hangzhou is known as "Paradise on Earth" for its beauty as well as renowned as “Home of Silk”, “Tea Capital”, “Town of Fish and Rice” for its rich resources.
